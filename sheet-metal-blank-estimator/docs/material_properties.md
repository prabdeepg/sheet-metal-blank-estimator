## Minimum Bend Radius Notes
- Bending below minimum radius causes cracking on the outer surface
- For 6061-T6: anneal to T0 temper before tight bends, then re-heat-treat
- For titanium: warm forming (150–300°C) reduces springback and cracking risk
- Always add at minimum a 0.5×t radius to avoid sharp corner stress concentration
